about us

Retrip is a Tunisia based tour operator, which specializes in group travel . Our main target niches and incoming travel from Africa and around the world. Our tours are packed with fun experiences, get-together, making new friends and doing this affordably, yet with high service standards from the beginning to the end. Our team consists of young and enthusiast travel professionals, those take the best care of our wonderful passengers!
